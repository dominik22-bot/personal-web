Core Philosophy

Most marketing teams treat performance and brand as opposing forces — one is science, the other is art. I reject that framing entirely.

Performance marketing without brand rents attention. It works until it doesn't, and you're always at the mercy of auction dynamics and algorithm changes.

Brand without performance is faith-based marketing. Beautiful, perhaps, but impossible to optimize and hard to defend in a boardroom.

The connective tissue between them is data literacy — the ability to ask the right questions, build measurement frameworks that reflect reality, and make decisions that serve both the quarter and the decade.
